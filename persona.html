Meet Our Learners & Stories

Feng Lin

"The quiet voice behind powerful stories."

Feng Lin is a 17-year-old student from rural Guizhou who experiences learning variation due to anxiety and self-expression. Although he loves literature and dreams of studying abroad, traditional classrooms make it difficult for him to share personal stories. Through 1-on-1 private tutoring with instructors who are sensitive to emotional and mental well-being, Feng gradually finds his voice and crafts compelling application essays.

Yue Wang

"She writes what others struggle to say."

Yue Wang is an 18-year-old high school graduate from rural Gansu who expresses learning variation in spoken communication but demonstrates exceptional strength in writing. Her local classes rely heavily on oral participation, limiting her engagement. Through text-based forums and writing-focused activities, she shines through her words and gains confidence in pursuing scholarships.

Gang Zhao

"A mind in motion, focused through design."

Gang Zhao is an 18-year-old student from a rural county in Gansu who navigates learning variation due to attention and task persistence. He is passionate about science but often finds long, traditional assignments difficult to manage. With modular, high-intensity lessons and multimedia support, he stays engaged and successfully completes his essays.
