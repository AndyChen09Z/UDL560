Meet Our Learners & Stories

Feng Lin

"The quiet voice behind powerful stories."

Feng Lin is a 17-year-old student from rural Guizhou with generalized anxiety disorder. While he loves literature and dreams of studying abroad, he finds it difficult to share personal stories in traditional classrooms. Through 1-on-1 private tutoring with mental health–aware instructors, Feng begins to express his inner world and craft compelling application essays.

Yue Wang

"She writes what others struggle to say."

Yue Wang is an 18-year-old high school graduate from rural Gansu with a speech and language disorder. Her local classes are largely oral, limiting her ability to participate. However, with a text-based forum and writing-centered activities, she shines through her words and gains confidence in applying for scholarships.

Gang Zhao

"A mind in motion, focused through design."

Gang Zhao is an 18-year-old student from a county in Gansu diagnosed with ADHD. He’s passionate about science but often struggles with long, traditional assignments. The modular, high-intensity lessons and multimedia support in our program help him stay focused and complete his essays on time.
